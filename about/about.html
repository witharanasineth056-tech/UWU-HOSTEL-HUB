Our Story

Finding a suitable hostel can be one of the biggest challenges for students starting university. Hostel Finder was created to help Uva Wellassa University students easily discover safe, affordable, and comfortable hostels near the campus. Instead of searching through different sources, students can explore hostel information in one convenient place.

Our goal is to make the hostel search simple, reliable, and stress-free, helping every student find a place where they can feel safe, comfortable, and ready to focus on their university journey.
